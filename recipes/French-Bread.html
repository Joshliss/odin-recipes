Fantastic French Bread

I’m making an easy French bread recipe that anyone can make in no time! With just 6 ingredients and 3 hours, you can make this easy homemade French bread at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water 110-120 degrees
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2½ teaspoons yeast
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1½ teaspoons salt
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 egg beaten
  • tbsp cornmeal

Cooking Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl add sugar and water. Stir to dissolve sugar and sprinkle with yeast. Let set 5 minutes or until foamy.
  2. Add oil, salt and half of the flour. Stir to combine.
  3. Change to dough hook if your using a mixer and add remaining flour. If you are not using a mixer that's okay just use a wooden spoon.
  4. Pull out and knead into a ball.
  5. Place in greased bowl flipping to cover both side.
  6. Cover and let rise 1 hour.
  7. Punch down and let it rise again for about 30 minutes.
  8. Pull out and flatten out into a rectangle.
  9. Jelly" roll it up and tuck the ends under pinching gently.
  10. Grease a baking sheet and sprinkle with cornmeal. Place loaf on baking sheet
  11. Cover and let rise 30-60 minutes.
  12. Pre heat oven to 375 degrees. Brush the top of the loaf with egg wash.
  13. ive it a couple quick swipes with a serrated knife.
  14. Bake for 25 minutes.
